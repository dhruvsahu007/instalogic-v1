Sample Questions for Instalogic Website 
1.  General / About the company 
●  Who is Instalogic? 
●  What does Instalogic do? 
●  Where are you located? 
●  When was Instalogic founded? 
●  Who are the founders or leadership team? 
●  What is your company mission? 
●  Do you have any certifications? 
2.  Services overview 
●  What services do you offer? 
●  Tell me about your Data Analytics services. 
●  What is Business Intelligence Support & Advisory? 
●  Do you offer Financial Impact Assessment services? 
●  Tell me about your Software Development capabilities. 
●  Do you provide Training and Skilling programs? 
●  What e-governance solutions do you provide? 
●  What is Business Process Reengineering (BPR) at Instalogic? 
3.  Case studies / Past work 
●  Can you show me your case studies? 
●  Tell me about the Visual Analytics Dashboard for MBOCWWB. 
●  What did you deliver for the Cess Collection Portal? 
●  Can I see the Financial Impact Assessment you did for Mahajyoti & OBBW? 
●  Do you have government client examples? 
4.  Project scoping & engagement 
●  How do I start a project with Instalogic? 
●  What is your typical project engagement model (fixed-price, T&M)? 
●  Can you help write an RFP or proposal? 
●  What are your typical project timelines? 
●  Do you offer post-deployment support and maintenance? 
●  How do you handle project change requests? 
5.  Technical & delivery questions 
●  Which BI tools do you use (Power BI, Qlik, Tableau)? 
●  Which programming languages and frameworks do you use? 
●  Do you integrate with existing databases (Postgres, MySQL, Excel)? 
●  Can you connect dashboards to live data sources? 
●  What is your approach to data security and privacy? 
●  Do you provide APIs for data access? 
●  How do you manage deployments and DevOps? 
6.  Pricing & commercial 
●  How much does a dashboard project cost? 
●  Do you offer fixed cost or subscription pricing? 
●  Do you provide cost estimates or quotations? 
●  What payment methods do you accept? 
●  Do you offer discounts for government projects or long-term contracts? 
7.  Demos, trials & PoC 
●  Can I request a demo of your dashboards? 
●  Do you provide a proof-of-concept (PoC)? 
●  How long does a PoC take and what does it include? 
●  Can I get a sandbox to test the solution? 
8.  Support & training 
●  How do I get technical support? 
●  What are your support SLAs? 
●  Do you offer user training sessions for dashboards? 
●  Can you provide training materials or certification for staff? 
9.  Careers & hiring 
●  Are you hiring? 
●  What job openings are available now? 
●  How do I apply to work at Instalogic? 
●  What is life like at Instalogic? (WFH, culture) 
●  How can I submit my resume? 
10. Contact & communication 
●  How can I contact Instalogic? 
●  What is your phone number and email? 
●  Can I schedule a call with sales? 
●  Where is your office located? 
●  How long will it take to get a response? 
11. Legal, privacy & compliance 
●  Where can I read your Privacy Policy and Terms of Use? 
●  Do you comply with GDPR/CCPA? 
●  How do you handle client data and confidentiality? 
●  Do you have ISO/CMMI certifications? 
12. Proposal / procurement specific 
●  Can you provide an estimated timeline and T&M estimate for a dashboard project 
with 5 reports? 
●  We have an RFP — how do we share it with you? 
●  Do you provide pre-bid clarifications for government tenders? 
●  Can you sign an NDA? 
13. Custom requests & integrations 
●  Can you build custom applications for our organization? 
●  Do you integrate with payment gateways or portals? 
●  Can you process bulk uploads from Excel templates? 
●  Can you do drone imagery and GIS marking projects? 
●  Do you provide AR/visualization hosting for survey data? 
14. Blog & insights queries 
●  What blog posts do you have about data analytics? 
●  Do you publish case studies or whitepapers? 
●  How can I subscribe to your newsletter? 
15. FAQ / quick-help prompts (short utterances) 
●  Services? (maps to list of services) 
●  Case studies? (maps to case studies page) 
●  Book demo 
●  Contact sales 
●  Support 
●  Careers 
16. Fallback / hand-off triggers (examples) 
●  I want to speak to a human. 
●  My issue is urgent — connect me to support. 
●  This is a procurement query — connect me to the proposals team. 
●  I want a custom quote — request contact details and project brief. 
17. Rich-actionable responses (suggested bot actions for the above) 
●  Provide page link (e.g., Our Services, Case Studies, Contact Us). 
●  Offer immediate form to "Request Demo" / "Request PoC" (collect project name, 
brief, contact). 
●  Provide phone/email and ask if user wants a callback. 
●  If question is technical, offer to collect requirement doc or schedule technical call. 
●  If user asks for careers, offer "Upload Resume" form. 
●  For legal/data queries, provide link to Privacy Policy/Terms. 
18. Example multi-turn flows (for training) A. Demo request flow 
●  User: "I want a demo of your analytics dashboard." 
●  Bot: "Sure — what industry is the dashboard for? (Govt / Finance / Retail / Other)" 
●  Bot: Collect: industry, contact name, email, preferred date/time → Confirm demo and 
create ticket. 
Notes for vendor/POC implementer: 
●  Map the questions above to intents & entities (e.g., service_type, case_study_name, 
contact_method). 
●  Provide quick reply buttons for top actions: "Request Demo", "Contact Sales", "View 
Services", "Upload RFP". 
●  Design fallback to collect user contact and escalate to human when confidence < 
threshold. 
●  Seed the bot answers with copy from Instalogic pages (Our Story, Our Services, 
Case Studies, Contact) and use links to the original pages for “read more”. 
●  For transactional flows (book demo, request PoC, upload RFP) implement a simple 
form flow (collect name, email, phone, short description) and confirm with a ticket ID. 
End of question bank.
